3rd-level enchantment

**Class**: Bard, Sorcerer Warlock, Wizard
**Casting Time**: 1 action
**Range**: 60 ft. (10 ft. radius)
**Components**: V, S
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You offer a dangerous bargain. Each enemy creature in range makes a Wisdom saving
throw. On a failed save, the creature takes 4d10 Fire or Cold damage (caster chooses)
and becomes Charmed. On success, it takes half damage and isn't Charmed.

Once per turn as a Bonus Action, you choose one Charmed creature to suggest a
course of activity described in 10 or fewer words. The activity must sound achievable
and not cause damage to the target or its allies. Each target pursues the suggestion to the best of its ability. The suggested activity can continue the entire duration, but if the suggested activity
can be completed in a shorter time, the spell ends for a target upon completing it. Each creature that has not been issued a command may attempt to save again at the end of each turn.
