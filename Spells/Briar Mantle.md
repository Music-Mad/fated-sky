1st-level abjuration

**Class**: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
**Casting Time**: 1 action
**Range**: Self
**Components**: V, S, M (a briar thorn)
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute

A protective briar winds and twists around you, thick with thorns and flowers. You gain 5 Temporary Hit Points. If a creature hits you with a melee attack roll before the spell ends, roll to determine the briar mantle effects.


| 1d10 | Effects                                                                           |
| ---- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | Blight ...the creature takes 5 Poison damage.                                     |
| 2    | Bright ...the creature takes 5 Fire damage.                                       |
| 3    | Cite ...the creature must state truthfully their next intended action aloud.      |
| 4    | Fight ...the creature must attack a new target on its next attack                 |
| 5    | Flight ...the creature's weapon turns into butterflies until their next turn.     |
| 6    | Sleight ...you may immediately move 5ft. without provoking an Opportunity Attack. |
| 7    | Smite ...the creature takes 5 Radiant damage.                                     |
| 8    | Spite ...the creature takes 5 Psychic damage.                                     |
| 9    | White ...the creature takes 5 Cold damage.                                        |
| 10   | Wight ...the creature takes 5 Necrotic damage.                                    |
**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot**
The Temporary Hit Points and the damage both increase by 5 for each spell slot level above 1. Effects that don't do damage don't have additional effect.
