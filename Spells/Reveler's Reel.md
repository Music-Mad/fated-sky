2nd-level enchantment

**Class**: Bard, Sorcerer Warlock, Wizard
**Casting Time**: 1 action
**Range**: 90 ft.
**Components**: V S, M (tiny silver bell)
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You cause one creature you can see within range to begin dancing uncontrollably. The
target must make a Wisdom saving throw. Ona failed save, the creature begins to dance in
place and has Disadvantage on attack rolls and Dexterity saving throws. A dancing creature can
move, but its Speed is halved, and it cannot take Reactions. On a successful save, the creature chooses a new target within 30 ft. That target gets no saving throw and begins dancing.

Alternatively, a target may voluntarily choose to fail the saving throw and dance for their turn. At the end of their next turn, they can pass the dancing along as if they had saved successfully,

At the end of a dancing creature's turn, the affected creature chooses another target within 30 ft. The targeted creature makes a Wisdom saving throw, and success and failure are resolved again.

If no creature is within range at any point, the spell ends.
