**Class**: bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
**Casting Time**: 1 action
**Range**: 30 ft
**Components**: V, S
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You touch one creature. The target must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failure, once before the spell ends, when the target makes an ability check, you can subtract 1d4 from the roll (no action required). You can choose to apply this penalty after seeing the roll but before the DM declares the result. The spell then ends.
